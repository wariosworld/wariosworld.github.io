Percentage Clock

This app allows you to reference time as a percentage. It converts the the 24 hour clock to a percentage. This app calculates time by the thousandth percent and updates in real time. Features a pulsing animation and modern design. Includes a reference guide to gauge your time throughout the day. You can add a widget to track your time without opening the app. Works with Apple Watch, iPhone, iPad.
