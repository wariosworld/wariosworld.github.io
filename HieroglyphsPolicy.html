My Name in Hieroglyphics Privacy Policy

"My Name in Hieroglyphics" is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information of our users. Information Collection and Use: "My Name in Hieroglyphics" does not collect any personal information from its users. All names and messages entered into the app are processed locally on the user's device and are not shared with anyone else. Data Security: We use appropriate technical measures to secure the user's data stored on their device. However, the user is responsible for protecting their device and the information stored on it. User Control: Users have full control over their names and messages and can delete them at any time. Changes to this Policy: "My Name in Hieroglyphics" reserves the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify users of any changes to this policy via our app or email.
